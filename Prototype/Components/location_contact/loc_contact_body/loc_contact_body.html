St. Luke’s Plastic & Reconstructive Surgery - Bethlehem

Our fellowship trained plastic and reconstructive surgeons are skilled in providing advanced procedures for men, women and children. Whether you want to restore what injury or illness took away, correct a birth defect or minimalize the effect of age, we will work with you to help you achieve your desired results. Our goal is to help you look, function and feel your best.

St. Luke’s Plastic & Reconstructive Surgery is part of St. Luke’s University Health Network, the leading provider of primary and specialty care in the greater Lehigh Valley and Warren County, New Jersey. We work closely with specialists in nearly every area of medicine – especially oncologists and orthopedic, bariatric and trauma surgeons – to ensure your health and safety and a gratifying experience.
